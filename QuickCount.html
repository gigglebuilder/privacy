QuickCount Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how QuickCount handles your personal information in accordance with applicable privacy laws and regulations. We are committed to protecting user privacy and ensuring transparency in how data is handled.

Personal Data Collection

QuickCount does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app operates entirely on the user's device and does not send any data to external servers. No personally identifiable information (PII) is processed or shared.

Third-Party Data Sharing

As QuickCount does not collect any personal information, there is no data shared or provided to third parties under any circumstances.
